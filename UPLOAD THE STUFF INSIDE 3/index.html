About Us

The Wine Country Cocktail Company is a locally owned mobile bar for hire in the Napa Valley. The company is a small family owned business. The mobile bar itself is made from a restored horse trailer and is decorated to accommodate any event. Wine Country Cocktail Co. is also able to provide a fully customizable bar experience and cocktail menu, complete with a fully educated bartending staff.
